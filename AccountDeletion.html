Delete your account/h1>

Last updated: January 26, 2026

We understand that managing your digital footprint is important. We offer two methods for deleting your account from our app:

1. In-App Deletion:

This is the quickest and most straightforward method.

To delete your account directly within the app, please follow below steps:

Open the FastCalo AI app and navigate to the Profile -> Settings -> Account settings -> Delete Account.

When you tap on Delete Account, a confirmation popup will appear, stating: "Are you sure you want to delete your account? This action cannot be undone".

To proceed, tap the Delete. Please note, this action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, you will not be able to recover or access any data associated with it, as it will be permanently erased.

2. Email Request for Account Deletion

Alternatively, you may request the deletion of your account by sending an email to hello.fastcaloai@gmail.com. Please be aware that this process may take a few days, depending on the current volume of customer support inquiries. We are committed to assisting you with your request as promptly as possible. Thank you for using FastCalo AI.
